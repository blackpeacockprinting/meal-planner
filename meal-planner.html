Bell Pepper Pasta + Chicken
⏱ ~12 minutes
Ingredients
  • 1 bell pepper
  • ¼ pkg Half & Half (dairy)
  • ½ pkg pasta
  • 2 pieces chicken
  • 1 tsp oil
  • Garlic salt, pepper
Notes
  • Season chicken well before airfrying
Steps
Cut bell pepper small, fry in pan with 1 tsp oil until brown
Add Half & Half, garlic salt, pepper — stir
Season chicken & cook in airfryer
Cook pasta. Done! 💙
🍝
Spaghetti Bolognese
⏱ ~10 min + freeze extra
Ingredients
  • Minced meat
  • 1 big can diced tomatoes
  • 1 small can tomato sauce
  • Frozen diced onions
  • Italian seasoning or German pasta fix (seasoning packet)
  • Garlic powder
  • Pasta
  • 1 tsp oil
Notes
  • Freeze leftover sauce 3–4 months
Steps
Brown meat & onions with 1 tsp oil
Add salt, pepper, garlic powder, Italian seasoning / pasta fix packet — mix well
Add both cans of tomatoes, mix well
Cook pasta. Freeze leftover sauce. Done! 💙

Rice, Broccoli + Marinated Chicken
⏱ Easy weeknight
Ingredients
  • Rice
  • Frozen broccoli
  • Marinated chicken (pre-frozen)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Garlic salt
Notes
  • Use pre-marinated chicken from freezer
Steps
Cook rice
Cook broccoli in pot, add 1 tsp sesame oil + garlic salt
Cook marinated chicken in airfryer. Done! 💙

Tuna Helper + Cauliflower
⏱ One-pot easy
Ingredients
  • ½ pkg pasta
  • 1 pouch tuna in water
  • ½ pkg frozen cauliflower
  • ¼ pkg Half & Half (dairy)
  • Quattro Formaggi (4-cheese mix)
  • Onion, garlic salt, pepper
Notes
  • Cook pasta & cauliflower in same pot
Steps
Cook pasta ~7 min, add cauliflower to water, finish cooking together — drain
Mix tuna, Half & Half, onion, garlic salt, pepper in pot
Add pasta & cauliflower, stir in Quattro Formaggi. Done! 💙
🍤
Rice, Shrimp, Carrots & Mandu
⏱ Quick & flavourful
Ingredients
  • Rice
  • ½ small pkg shrimp
  • 1 big carrot
  • Frozen Mandu dumplings
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic salt, garlic powder
  • ½ tsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp butter (optional)
Notes
  • Butter adds richness to shrimp
Steps
Cook rice
Cook shrimp in 1 tsp sesame oil, add garlic salt, garlic powder, ½ tsp soy sauce (+ butter if available)
Cut carrot in thin strips, add ½ tsp sesame oil + garlic salt
Prepare Mandu. Done! 💙
🍳
Leftover Egg Fried Rice
⏱ Zero-waste meal
Ingredients
  • Leftover rice
  • 1 egg
  • Leftover veg (carrots, peas…)
  • Leftover shrimp or chicken
  • ½ tsp + ½ tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • Garlic salt, garlic powder
Notes
  • Perfect Sunday clean-out meal
Steps
Fry egg in ½ tsp sesame oil until ¾ cooked — remove from pan
Fry rice, veggies & protein in ½ tsp sesame oil
Add 1 tsp soy sauce, garlic salt, garlic powder — mix well
Add egg back. Done! 💙

Pasta, Asparagus + Sausage
⏱ ~20 minutes
Ingredients
  • ½ pkg pasta
  • 1 pkg frozen asparagus
  • Hillshire sausage
  • ¼ pkg Half & Half (dairy) or Quattro Formaggi
  • Garlic salt, pepper, oil
Notes
  • Half & Half and Quattro Formaggi are interchangeable here
Steps
Cook pasta
Slice sausage & fry in ½ tsp oil
Defrost asparagus in microwave ~2 min, drain & cut into small pieces
Fry asparagus with ½ tsp oil, add Half & Half or Quattro Formaggi, garlic salt, pepper
Mix pasta with sauce, top with sausage. Done! 💙

Gimbap
⏱ Weekend project
Ingredients
  • Rice
  • Surimi sticks
  • Cucumber
  • ¼ carrot
  • 1 egg
  • Nori (seaweed) sheets
  • 1 tsp sesame oil, ½ tsp salt
  • Garlic salt
Notes
  • Cool rice with cling wrap on top
  • Flip egg before fully cooked through
Steps
Cook rice, add 1 tsp sesame oil + ½ tsp salt. Cool with cling wrap on top
Cut cucumber in strips; cut carrot in thin strips + add garlic salt
Scramble egg, cook in ½ tsp sesame oil, flip before fully cooked through
Assemble and roll Gimbap. Done! 💙

Curry
⏱ Cozy dinner
Ingredients
  • Rice
  • Curry seasoning package
  • Chicken, cut into pieces
  • Peas, carrots, potatoes, cauliflower
  • ½ tsp sesame oil
Notes

Add vegetables, then add curry package. Done! 💙

Beef Stroganoff Pasta
⏱ Hearty dinner
Ingredients
  • Minced meat
  • Onions
  • ½ pkg pasta
  • Quattro Formaggi (4-cheese mix)
  • Broccoli / cauliflower / peas
  • ½ tsp oil
Notes
  • Any veg of your choice works
Steps
Brown minced meat in ½ tsp oil, add onions
Add vegetables of choice
Stir in Quattro Formaggi
Cook pasta. Done! 💙
